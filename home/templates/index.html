Welcome to Rustomjee Ocean Vista

Rustomjee Ocean Vista is an upcoming sea touch residential community in Versova. It offers 3/4/5 BHK luxurious and spacious apartments and a provision of Jodi apartments. The Vastu-compliant residences are equipped with the finest design, contemporary features and branded fixtures. Moreover, this gated community presents a wide variety of modern amenities like a swimming pool, banquet hall, banquet hall, alfresco lounge, gymnasium, multipurpose lawn, jogging track, kids’ play area and more.

The neighbourhood has every facility such as schools, hospitals, shopping malls and commercial parks. Orchids The International School, Kokilaben Dhirubhai Ambani Hospital, St. Antony’s High School, Belle Vue Multispeciality Hospital, Pinnacle Business Park, Dmart and Infiniti Mall are nearby. The Versova metro station and Juhu Versova Link Road offer smooth connectivity to the central suburbs and South Mumbai. Both domestic and international airports are just a 30-minute drive away.
