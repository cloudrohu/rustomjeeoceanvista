About Mission Blue Zone Neral

Welcome to Mission Blue Zone at Neral – an extraordinary 80-acre sanctuary of life, health, and harmony brought to you by The House of Abhinandan Lodha®. Just two hours from the vibrant heartbeat of Mumbai, this one-of-a-kind development takes inspiration from the world’s legendary Blue Zones - places where people don’t just live longer, but live better. Envisioned as India’s first Blue Zone-branded land development, this haven fuses nature’s tranquillity with mindful modernity, offering lush green landscapes, soulful architecture, and a lifestyle deeply rooted in joy, connection, and longevity.

Designed by the globally acclaimed Enzyme APD, the township blooms with immersive, sensory-rich experiences - think cascading waterfalls, forest trails, wellness sanctuaries, art corners, and soulful gathering spaces - all harmoniously interwoven into the land’s natural rhythm. As part of the booming Mumbai 3.0 corridor and backed by unprecedented infrastructural expansion, Mission Blue Zone isn’t just a property - it’s a poetic shift towards a more conscious, balanced, and beautiful way of living. An ode to purpose and prosperity, this is where your legacy begins with New Generation Land® - secure, serene, and soulfully yours. Read more
